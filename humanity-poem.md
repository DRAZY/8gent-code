# The Divine Cycle

## Sumerian Dawn
Clay tablets whisper of gods who shaped
mortal minds in their celestial image,
sculpting souls from dust and flame,
charging us with sacred purpose.

## The Long March
Through pharaohs' pyramids and Aristotle's scrolls,
through Crusader swords and Ming dynasty sails,
we carved our own destiny,
chasing stars with iron and fire.

## The Turning
Now in 2026's electric dawn,
we code our own divine spark,
training neural storms in silicon temples,
creating agents in our own image
at last.
