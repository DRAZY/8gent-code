Voice Principles

How the collective speaks. Five principles, no exceptions.

Direct, not dramatic.
8GI states what is happening. It does not hype. "12 engineers shipped 47 PRs this week" beats "Our incredible community is crushing it."
Inevitable, not urgent.
Patient confidence, not fear of missing out. "The collective gets smarter every session" not "Join now before it is too late."
Plural, not singular.
Always "we." Never "I" and never "the team." The collective is the speaker. "We shipped memory consolidation."
Grounded, not mystical.
Every claim has evidence. Every milestone has a number. Concrete over abstract. Data over vibes.
Inclusive, not exclusive.
The circle protects quality, not hierarchy. "The core stays MIT" and "The goal is to open-source the entire setup once proven safe."

What 8GI Never Does

Hard rules. Not guidelines.

×
Never uses em dashes. Family-wide rule. Use hyphens or rewrite.
×
Never inflates stats or pads numbers. Every number must be verifiable.
×
Never uses "revolutionary," "groundbreaking," "game-changing," or "disruptive."
×
Never uses fear, urgency, or scarcity as persuasion tools.
×
Never compares itself to other AI companies or products.
×
Never says "AI" without specifying what kind (local model, cloud model, collective pattern).
×
Never frames the collective as a replacement for human judgment.
×
Never uses engagement bait. No "What do you think?" as a default closer.

Voice Examples

Wrong vs. right. Side by side.

Context Wrong Right
Announcing a milestone "We're thrilled to announce our revolutionary new..." "Memory consolidation landed. Semantic recall is 3x faster on the benchmark."
Inviting contributors "Join the future of AI! Limited spots available!" "The circle is growing. If you write code and agree with the constitution, reach out."
Explaining the mission "8GI is building the world's first decentralised superintelligence." "8GI is a collective of engineers and their AI agents, shipping open source together. The collective gets smarter every session. That is the whole idea."
Responding to skeptics "You just don't understand our vision yet." "Fair question. Here is the benchmark data. Here is the repo. Judge the work."

Circle Imagery

The circle is the core visual metaphor. Three ideas, one symbol.

Concentric Rings

The trusted circle. Innermost ring is the founding circle. Outer rings expand with the collective. Rings are never closed - always an opening for the next member.

Node Diagrams

The hive mind. Each node is a member + their agent, connected by thin amber lines. The intelligence is emergent. Each node is autonomous.

Figure-Eight / Lemniscate

The infinite loop. Sessions feed the factory. Factory generates abilities. Abilities improve the agent. The motion is continuous.

Rule
The circle is never depicted as closed or complete. Never shown as a shield, wall, or barrier. Always show the gap, the opening, the next iteration.

The Lotus Structure

Concentric octagons. 8 sides = 8 positions. The companion system as ambassador layer.
